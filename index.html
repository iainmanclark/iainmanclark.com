Code Breaker Game

For the best experience, please play this game on a desktop computer or rotate your device to landscape mode.

How To Play

  • Important: Patterns may look very similar - differences can be as subtle as a slight position change or tiny detail!
  • Observe: Study every detail of the pattern carefully. Look for exact positions, sizes, and rotations.
  • Remember: The pattern will disappear after a few seconds - memorize every detail.
  • Compare: Look closely at all four options. Only one matches exactly!
  • Warning: Don't be fooled by similar patterns. They may differ by just one tiny detail.
  • Focus: As levels increase, differences become more and more subtle.
  • Score: Earn points based on speed and level - but accuracy is key!
  • Time: Work quickly but carefully. Better to take an extra second than choose wrong!
  • Challenge: Try to beat your high score by mastering pattern recognition.
